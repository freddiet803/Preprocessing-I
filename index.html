Jason Bourne - Special Agent

Jason Bourne was born as David Webb on September 13, 1970, in Nixa, Missouri, to Mary and Richard Webb. His father was killed in Beirut while working as a CIA station chief. Webb joined the U.S. Army Special Forces ("Green Berets"). He later went on to be inducted into the Army's Delta Force (1st Special Forces Operational Detachment-Delta or "1st SFOD-Delta"), where he attained the officer rank of Captain. Following his enlistment with Delta Force, Webb joined the CIA on January 4th, 1997 and was inducted into the Treadstone project where he was codenamed, Jason Bourne. He is a Catholic. His blood type is O- as stated on his dog tags. As Jason Bourne, his blood type states A+ as indicated on his Treadstone file
